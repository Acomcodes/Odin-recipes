classic-waffles

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week!

With just basic pantry staples like flour , eggs, and milk,these waffles require very little prep

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  5. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Creamy-Pesto-shrimp

This creamy pesto shrimp pasta is perfect for weeknight dinners and is one of our family's favorites.

This easy pesto shrimp pasta is packed with silky noodles and shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 pound linguine pasta
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ⅓ cup pesto
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients together.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in linguine and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in cream and season with pepper; cook, stirring constantly, for 6 to 8 minutes.
  4. Stir Parmesan cheese into cream sauce until thoroughly mixed. Stir in pesto and cook until thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in shrimp and cook until they turn pink, about 5 minutes. Serve sauce over hot linguine.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Starbucks Copycat Strawberries-and-Cream Cake Pops

These sweet berry cake treats will sweeten any special day

If you like, shape into hearts rather than triangles and omit the white and green piping—prefect for Valentine's Day

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1(15.25-ounce) box vanilla cake mix, plus ingredients on box to make cake
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup finely crushed freeze-dried strawberries

Coating

  • 7 ounces strawberry-flavor candy melts
  • 6 ounces red candy melts
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 ounces green candy melts
  • 3 ounces white candy melts

Steps

  1. Bake Cake: Prepare and bake cake mix in a 9x13-inch baking pan according to package directions, 25 to 35 minutes. Let cake cool slightly in pan until cool enough to handle, about 10 minutes.
  2. Combine powdered sugar, butter, vanilla, and salt in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with paddle attachment; beat at medium speed until creamy, about 1 minute, scraping down sides as needed. Crumble cake into bowl and add freeze-dried strawberry powder. Beat at low speed until mixture is combined and evenly moistened, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Shape Cake Pops: Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. Portion mixture into 30 (1 1/2-inch) balls using a #40 or 2-tablespoon cookie scoop. Shape each ball into a rounded triangle and gently insert a cake pop stick from bottom side of triangle; arrange on prepared baking sheet. Chill, covered, until firm, at least 30 minutes (or up to 1 day).
  4. Make Coating: Microwave strawberry- flavor candy melts and red candy melts in a medium 2-cup glass measuring cup on High, stirring every 15 seconds, until melted, about 2 minutes. Stir in oil until mixture is smooth and pourable.
  5. Working with 1 cake pop at a time, dip cake pop in strawberry-flavor candy melt mixture until evenly coated, allowing excess to drip off. Place cake pop upright by inserting bottom of stick into a Styrofoam block or cardboard box with small holes poked in it. Let stand until completely set and hard to the touch, about 15 minutes. Repeat with remaining cake pops and strawberry-flavor candy melt mixture. (If candy melt mixture begins to harden, microwave on High in 10-second increments as needed.)
